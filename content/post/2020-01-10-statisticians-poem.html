We are statisticians.

We may not be normal-

But we are transformable.

We are the “mean” lovers.

We feel complete and sufficient.

We don’t accept proposals, we just fail to reject.

We do things with confidence, frequency, and variability.

We can legally comment on someone’s posterior distribution.

We are always right because no one knows what we do.

We never have to say we are certain.

We get paid to make errors.

We are honestly significantly different.
